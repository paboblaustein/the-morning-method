The Morning Alignment

Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

The Morning Alignment is built to be private by design. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your information — ever.

What we store

Everything you enter in the app — your gratitude and powerless logs, and which steps you completed — is saved only on your own device. It never leaves your phone, and the developer has no way to see it, access it, or recover it.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, sign‑ins, or profiles.
  • No analytics, tracking, advertising, or third‑party services.
  • No servers receiving your entries. There is nothing to leak.

Your control

You can copy your logs to your own Notes or journal at any time — that's you moving your own data, under your control. Deleting the app removes all of its stored data from your device.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page. Questions can be sent to the developer contact listed on the app's App Store page.
