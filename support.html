Support

The Morning Alignment is a four-step morning practice — Sit, Name, Read, Recognize & Release. It is deliberately simple, and most questions answer themselves inside the app. A few that don't:

Where is my data?

On your device, and only there. There are no accounts and no servers. Your gratitude and powerless logs live privately on your phone; you can copy either log to your own Notes at any time from the Logs screen.

I got a new phone — where are my logs?

Because nothing is stored off-device, logs don't transfer by themselves. Before switching phones, open Logs and copy each log to your Notes — that's your backup.

Why doesn't the app remind or nag me?

By design. No streaks, no badges, no notifications inside the practice. The routine is the reward. If a day slips, the calendar just waits.

What's the Minimum Version?

The whole sequence at its smallest dose (about six minutes) for the days that would otherwise be skipped. Keep the sequence; lower the dose.
